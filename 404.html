Tea with Santa

Tea with Santa is a community event in Walkden and Little Hulton, UK. Please remember that the team is entirely made up of volunteers who are passionate about making the difference where we live. We will always aim to respond to emails within 48 hours, but it may take slightly longer depending on personal circumstance. Children must ALWAYS be accompanied by a responsible adult.

In the event that you, or your child fail to listen to our instructions after repeated warnings we reserve the right to ask you and your children leave the event. No refunds will be given in this instance.
