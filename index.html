About

Learn More About Us

Spreading Christmas Joy!

Tea with Santa was born in the summer of 2021 when members of St. John's the Baptist Church proposed to run a secular Christmas Event that aimed to build community spirit and bring people together after the COVID lockdowns.

St. John's Facebook Page

Volunteer Run

Members of the Tea with Santa Team are members of the congretation at St. John's and take part to spread Christmas Joy!

Not for Profit

We do not operate Tea with Santa for profit. Any money that is left over is donated to St. John's so they can keep the Community Center open!

For the Community

Everything we do has the Community at heart. We want to build a strong community and spread joy this festive season.

Welcoming All

We welcome all families to Tea with Santa. Families of all faiths and no faiths are always welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Tea with Santa take place?

Tea with Santa takes place in St. John's (Ellesemere) Community Center. We're opposite the large church on Algernon Road. Our full formal address is: St. John's Community Center, Algernon Road, Walkden, Worsley, MANCHESTER, M28 3RE.

Do adults and babies need to pay?

Accompanying adults and children under 1 are completely free. Accompanying adults recieve complimentary tea, coffee and cake, and babies can recieve a free personalized ornament if we are provided with their name when booking. Adults can pay an additional £3 for a meal of their own, which will be served at the same time as their children's meals.

Why do we ask for your children's names when booking?

All children will recieve a personalized Christmas Ornament (that is hand made) as a part of their gift bag. No personal information is stored, or sold on by the Tea with Santa Team.

Can I bring additional children with me on the day without booking in advance?

No. To ensure there is as little waste as possible we prepare the right amount of food for how many guests have booked on the event. All gifts (including personalized ones) are prepared in advance. We also release a limited amount of tickets for each event to ensure we remain within the fire safety limits for the Community Center.

Can you accomodate people with special dietary requirements?

We will do our best to accomodate all people and their needs. Our kitchen is safely and securely cleaned before and after each event. There are no nuts present in the kitcken, but other groups do use the kitchen throughout the week, there may be traces of nuts remaining in spite of our best efforts to remove them. If you have any requirements, please contact us before booking to make us aware.

I've made separate bookings for my children and want them all sat together, or I want to make sure I am sat near another group that have booked. Is this possible?

To ensure family groups are sat together, we loosely create a seating chart. If you want to be sat near another group, send us an email once the booking has been made and we will make sure you are sat with the people you want.

I need to ammend or cancel my booking. What do I do?

Please contact us, and we'll be more than happy to help!
